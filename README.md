# Product Launch Go-To-Market (GTM) Strategy Plan

## Project Overview

This project presents a complete Go-To-Market (GTM) Strategy Plan for launching a new product in a competitive market. The objective is to simulate how business analysts, product managers, strategy teams, and marketing professionals evaluate market opportunities, identify target customers, analyze competitors, develop pricing strategies, forecast revenue, and design launch plans to maximize product success.

The project follows a structured framework covering market research, customer segmentation, positioning, pricing, distribution, promotion planning, and performance monitoring through an executive Power BI dashboard.

---

# Objective

The primary objective of this project is to design a comprehensive GTM strategy that helps organizations successfully launch a product while minimizing risk and maximizing market adoption.

Key objectives include:

* Understanding market demand and opportunities
* Identifying target customer segments
* Evaluating competitor positioning
* Developing effective pricing strategies
* Forecasting demand and revenue
* Planning distribution and marketing channels
* Tracking launch readiness through analytics dashboards

---

# Business Problem

Many product launches fail due to:

* Poor understanding of customer needs
* Incorrect market positioning
* Weak competitive differentiation
* Ineffective pricing strategies
* Inadequate marketing planning
* Poor channel selection

This project addresses these challenges by creating a structured GTM framework that enables data-driven launch decisions.

# Revenue Forecasting Methodology

Revenue forecasting was performed using expected conversion and pricing assumptions.

### Formula

Revenue Forecast = Expected Conversions × Product Price

### Additional Analysis

* Demand forecasting
* Revenue scenarios
* Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC)
* Break-even analysis

### Break-Even Formula

Break-Even Units = Fixed Costs ÷ (Selling Price − Variable Cost per Unit)

This methodology helped estimate future sales performance and profitability.

Affordable pricing improved projected adoption rates.

Digital channels generated the highest expected reach and conversions.

# Strategic Recommendations
